Remembering Us

"Remembering Us" is a conceptualized, site-specific piece meant to create an introspective experience for tourists and local residents alike. It would be located at Christopher Columbus Waterfront Park in Boston, Massachusetts and moderated by a volunteer with these instructions.

Passersby would be encouraged to write their favorite memory on a tag made of plantable paper and tie it to the nearby trees or arches.
